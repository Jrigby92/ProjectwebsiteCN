My Story

Over the past couple of years I’ve been craving a career path thats a bit more grounded. Simultaneously, around 2 years ago I stumbled across a book called ‘Sapiens’ by yuval Noah Harari. It documented the birth of civilisation and traced the steps from that point until the present day. This book was sequaled by another book called "Homo Deus", which listed potential directions of the future of civilisation. I found all of this absoltuley fascinating ! Namely, it was the progress and the enormous potential of AI, along with the impact it could have on the future which startled me most. From that point I began to research coding / developing as a career. I loved the fact work can be done remotely and that career opportunities are so plentiful. When the COVID-19 epidemic broke out, it felt like the perfect time to make the switch and I was lucky enough to find Ezra, who enrolled me on this fantastic course at Codenation.
