2.1.1 Describing the content and layout for each page


I have tried to break the content down in a way that makes it easily communicable and also very engaging to the user.


In detail

The content for each page consists of my answers for each unit point. I have tried to break the content down in a way that make it easily communicable and also very engaging to the user. I have done this by breaking up the questions into separate sections of each webpage. I have also separated the most salient point from each answer and placed it above the rest of the text. In the hope that this will give the user a clear feel for what the remaining content will consist of and what it’s trying to say. This is a method commonly used in recent times by several major news outlets, to create engaging and eye grabbing web content.

In terms of stylisation I have organised each webpage in a unique way, this is for several reasons. Firstly, given that the whole website is a portfolio for my technical and coding abilities I wanted to create the best looking and most technically difficult website that I could, within the timespan available. Ultimately, so the website will be fit for its purpose and create a good impression for future employers. Secondly, I wanted to give the user a fun experience where each page feels fresh and like anew adventure. I wanted the user to be excited about discovering the rest of the website. I can’t say that I have fulfilled this aim, however I believe that through aiming for this, I will have produced better content than otherwise.
