Review the benefits and drawbacks of IT tools and systems used in terms of productivity and efficiency – could you have been more efficient when making your website? How? Could you have done it in teams?











A major benefit that has encompassed all of the tools and systems we’ve learned are that they are easy to understand and adapt to.

Much thought has obviously gone into visual studio code, this is clear in all of the small and seemingly irrelevant features such as predicting the function/object/property etc that you will choose next. These small features build up to actually make the editor quite efficient. I would like to see a web preview feature in there however, as I believe this would make the sriram more efficient. I would also like to contest the efficiency of the ‘scrum’ method of creating a to do list, moving it to doing and then finally to done on the trello board. For a relatively small project like this one which is being carried out in a linear fashion and by a single person, I believe it’s easy to keep track of objectives without a board. It certainly makes the workload more cumbersome in my experience.

However, I do believe this project would have completed more efficiently in a team, where objectives could be completed in parallel with each other. This would enhance the ability of keeping unto speed with the project by getting an understanding of how long it takes to carry out each task from the beginning. In a team scenario, a trello board and the ‘Scrum method’ will have been much more appropriate and efficient also.
